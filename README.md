## Double Check

## Description:

Double Check is a web application aimed to make it easier for users to undertand ingredients and drug information behind everday products to improve safety awareness. Many consumers struggle to identify what ingredients are harmful, leading to confusion, misinforamtion, and fear based assumptions about health and nutrition.

The application aims to mitigate this problem by empowering users to search for productsand review reliable information from the Food and Drug Administration (FDA). The app provides users with clear product details, possible ingredient concerns, recalls, and real life adverse event inforamtion. This app will make product safetu inforamtion easier to access, understand, and trustworthy.

Key features also include being able to save products and incredients, view recent searches, and create customs lists for ingredients and drugs users want to avoid or increase intake. This makes the app more personalized and useful for health conscious consumers, individuals with allergies, and others who wish to increase awareness about products in their daily life.
